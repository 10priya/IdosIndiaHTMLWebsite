ABOUT US

IDOS is a financial platform that enables you to easily manage not just accounting, taxation and financial processes, but empowers you with data and curated reports for decision making and analytics. IDOS has been built on the premise that a technology solution must not just enable the business to prepare financial statements and file tax returns, which are consequential requirements and not the primary reason for which a business is set up. Rather, the technology must empower the business to become profitable through efficiency in operations and also by enabling the business to overcome key challenges including working capital management and ensuring reduced cost of sales. Further the technology must enable the business to engage effectively with the eco-system in which it operates including its customers, suppliers, staff and other stakeholders and such engagement must help the business to generate more profits. This is what IDOS delivers, in a simple and user friendly manner, through powerful eco-system engagement and by providing capabilities more than traditional accounting softwares and ERPs. IDOS has been built on a disruptive functional architecture which is empowering multiple businesses and also professional services providers across the world in their day to day business management.

CASE STUDIES

Case Study1

Sales & Customer accounting for a home appliances company

Task: Enabling a leading manufacturer of home appliances in managing their sales accounting process for their services business which constituted 80% of their annual revenue.

Remarks: Leading manufacturer of home appliances in the country has been using a global ERP for sales accounting and invoicing.However the ERP did not have a mobile version and needed another solution to manage mobile based sales engine for managing their sales in compliance with GST from mobile phone, generate GST compliant invoice & email it in real-time and also provide reports & analytics for their customer engagement and integrating the final output with the head office ERP.

IDOS Solution: IDOS sales and customer accounting module was customised to meet the customer requirements. IDOS built a bridge between the company's ERP and the sales executive who was meeting the customers and also the customers, whereby customers could raise service request, which was updated in the respective sales executives and service personnel mobile app and ensured that the service personnel reached the location, carried out the service, updated the usage of spares on the mobile which updated the spares inventory and also generated GST compliant invoice and emailed to the customer in a seamless manner.

Case Study2

Management of sales, inventory and direct benefit transfer for an agrochemical company

Task:Enabling a group of agrochemical companies in enhancing operational efficiencies in sales & collections process, management & accounting of Direct benefit transfer of subsidy and also better inventory management.

Remarks:A large agrochemical group was looking for a technology solution to help them in reducing turnaround time in inventory reconciliation, sales collections and also helping in operational efficiency in direct benefit transfer process, which consequently would result in more optimal working capital management. Their existing ERP was not nimble enough to facilitate engagement with the last mile where the sale was effected to the customer from the retailer.

IDOS Solution: IDOS provided an web based application with also a mobile app, which collated data from the ERP and pushed relevant data to the mobile app (IDOS) from where the field staff and sales staff could ascertain inventory and receivables position at the retailers in the geography where they were responsible. The mobile app with geotagging capabilities could identify the dealers at the location where the sales staff visited and alerted them to visit those retailers where receivables were overdue and also enables the staff to identify dealers and physically verify inventory and record in the mobile app, which in turn updated the inventory position in the ERP in real-time. All this helped the company in achieving operational efficiency, reduced receivables turnaround time and helped the company in better working capital management and rotation.

Task: Enabling an American manufacturing MNC in managing their distributor accounts, incentives to their distributors, sales incentives and accounting for all these components and post the output into their ERP.

Remarks: One of the world's largest industrial and household machine manufacturing organisation was looking for a solution to reduce the turnaround time with respect to computation and accounting of monthly distributor and sales related incentives, which was currently taking 7 working days time per month. The customer could not accomplish these complex computations and accounting in their ERP and the customisation of their ERP was cost prohibitive.

IDOS Solution: IDOS was able to accomplish this task and helped in reducing turnaround time, enhance positive engagement with distributors by making payments within 3 days of close of the month and also reduced the processing time from 7 working days to under a few minutes.This also reduced the cost of operations, since all computations and accounting was automated by IDOS and there was no need to hire external contractors for the task and also the time of sales and accounting professionals on this task was reduced by over 90%, enabling them to focus on their core activities and revenue generation and management.
